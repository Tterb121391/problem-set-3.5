Ultimate Frisbee

By: Brett Li


Ultimate frisbee is a small sport for now, but it has been rapidly growing over the past few years, and there are constantly more and more teams in every frisbee game and tournament.

So, What is Ultimate Frisbee?

When many people hear the word "frisbee," they usually think of a simple activity of catch and throw with friends or family, but what most people don't know is that frisbee is also a competitive sport, and when people refer the the competitive side of frisbee, it's called Ultimate frisbee.
